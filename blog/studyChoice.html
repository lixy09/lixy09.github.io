Study Choice

In this modern age of digital technology, computers and smartphones have become essential in daily life. While using them, I've noticed that some apps I need are a bit tricky to use. The idea of creating my own apps has always fascinated me, and I think it would be really rewarding. Plus, I'm tired of repetitive experiments and want to do something more creative and respontive. Building on my previous programming experience, I'm confident that this is the path I'm passionate about.

Before I made the decision of coming here, I’ve already started learning coding through online platforms like YouTube and freeCodeCamp, which have been helpful. However, I found it’s hard to write a real program by myself with just selfstudy. I believe that joining a formal school program will provide a more interactive learning experience, allowing me to communicate directly with teachers and classmates. Additionally, I see many exciting opportunities at HZ, like working on practical projects and connecting with companies. This is what motivates me as a college student.

After graduation, my main goal is to apply my knowledge and skills in software development to make a real impact in tech. In the long term, I envision a leadership role in an ICT company or or maybe even starting my own tech business, focusing on innovative solutions that benefit society. I'm excited to embark on this journey, armed with my ICT education, and work towards these ambitious career goals.
