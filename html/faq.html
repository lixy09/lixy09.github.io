 Frequently Asked Questions

 How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Go to https://hz.mynetpay.nl/ and then click on Webprint, select your file and click on upload.

 How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Check this page.

 How can you buy something (like when you sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Log into the web shop.

 How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Go to Self-Service Portal > Make a reservation > Reserve a room.

 What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Reveal your HZ pass at the barriers first, then you will be able to park in the lot.
